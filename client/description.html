Steel Quality Inspection Description

Label 1. Multiple Chips on the Surface

Small, scattered imperfections resembling chips, affecting the steel's smoothness and quality.

Label 2. Single Vertical Crack

A single long crack running vertically, weakening the steel's structural integrity.

Label 3. Multiple Vertical Cracks

Several vertical cracks present, compromising the steel's strength and durability.

Label 4. Multiple Large Surface Patches

Multiple extensive patches on the surface, indicating areas with potential weaknesses in the steel material.
